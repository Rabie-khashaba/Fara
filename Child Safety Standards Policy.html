Child Safety Standards Policy

Wanderr App is committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users.

We have a zero-tolerance policy against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE).

1. Prohibited Content

We strictly prohibit:

  • Any content involving child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Grooming, exploitation, or inappropriate interaction with minors

2. Reporting

Users can report any suspicious behavior through:

  • In-app reporting system
  • Email: info@wanderr-app.online

3. Enforcement

  • Immediate removal of violating content
  • Permanent ban of offending accounts
  • Reporting to relevant authorities

4. Compliance

We comply with all applicable child safety laws and regulations.

5. Contact

For CSAE concerns:

Email: info@wanderr-app.online
