Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 30, 2024

Welcome to Wander. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users:

  • Profile Information: When you register, we collect your name, username, email, and profile picture.
  • Location Data: Since Wander includes mapping and place discovery features, we may collect your precise or approximate location with your permission.
  • Content: Any places you save, reviews you write, or messages sent through the chat feature.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Wander app features.
  • Enable you to search for other users and discovery new places.
  • Facilitate social interactions and following other travelers.
  • Personalize your experience and provide relevant recommendations.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your profile information (Name, Username, Avatar) and public posts are visible to other users of the app to facilitate the social nature of the platform.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access.

5. Your Choices

You can update your profile information at any time within the app settings. You can also manage location permissions through your device's system settings.
